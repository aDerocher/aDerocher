My Artwork

I grew up skateboarding, and my favorite board to ride was always an Alien Workshop. I always really liked the graphics they had as well, and though I might take a stab at creating some in their style. So I drew up this "DNA" series and sent them a copy. I haven't seen them on the market... yet!

Another big influence on my artwork has been graffiti. I've been sketching my own pieces since middle school. This was one of my favorites I've done. I originally made the design on a wallet. I thought it made sense because thats what a wallet holds, all your capital. Once the wallet broke down, I cleaned up the design in illustrator and made some T-Shirts out of it.

While working in the HP design studio, I was proud to have contributed a design to this product. The small 'L' shaped indicators for ink cartridge placement were my design, and were proven the most effective graphic during user testing.

This take on the Gadsden Flag is Jordan Peterson inspired to say "You should always work to better yourself, take responsibility for your own life, and become the most formitable lobster you can".

My local climbing gym was having a T-Shirt design contest. This was my entry.

This was a design that I came up with as a tribute to my favorite author, economist Thomas Sowell.

A friend of mine invited me to help make some new logos for their band. I really liked these designs I came up with. (The band has since... disbanded)
